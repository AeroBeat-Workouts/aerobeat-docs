    Supporter Perks & Benefits¶

    AeroBeat operates on a "Support, Don't Buy" model. The core game is free, and becoming a Supporter is a voluntary contribution to help fund servers and development. In return, Supporters receive a suite of account-level perks focused on convenience, customization, and community status.

    1. Pricing¶

    Supporter tags are purchased as non-recurring products (time blocks).

    • 1 Month: $4.00
    • 2 Months: $8.00
    • 4 Months: $12.00 (Save 25%)
    • 6 Months: $16.00 (Save 33%)
    • 1 Year: $24.00 (Save 50%)
    • 18 Months: $39.00 (Save 46%)
    • 2 Years: $52.00 (Save 46%)

    2. Benefit Breakdown¶

    A. Social Status (The "Clout")¶

    Stand out in the community.

    • In-Game Badge: A unique "Supporter Heart" icon displayed next to your username on Leaderboards, Lobbies, and Profile.
    • Discord Role: Automatically syncs with the official Discord server to grant the "AeroBeat Supporter" role, unlocking private channels.
    • Profile Flair: Gold border and special visual effects on your Web Profile and In-Game Badge.

    B. Convenience & Limits¶

    Power-user features for heavy users.

    • Cloud Storage: Increased quota for UGC uploads (5GB vs 2GB for free users).
    • Friends List: Cap increased from 500 to 1000 friends.
    • Download Priority: Higher bandwidth limits for downloading mods from the CDN during peak traffic times.

    C. Cosmetic Exclusives¶

    Exclusive customization options.

    • Locker Room Access: Unlocks visibility of the "Supporter Collection" in the Avatar Shop.
      • Note: Items still require Workout Points (WP) to purchase.
    • Menu Customization: Ability to change the Main Menu background environment to any downloaded Environment mod (Free users are locked to the default).

    D. Community Influence¶

    Help shape the future of AeroBeat.

    • Weighted Voting: Supporter votes count double on "Feature Roadmap" polls.
    • Playlist Nominations: Ability to nominate playlists for the "Playlist of the Day" featured slot.

    E. Multiplayer Enhancements¶

    Express yourself in online lobbies.

    • Lobby Host Customization: When hosting a lobby, Supporters can change the visual environment (Skybox/Lighting) for all players in the waiting room.
    • Entrance FX: Unique particle effects (e.g., "Lightning Strike", "Digital Materialize") when joining a session.
    • Lobby Emotes: Access to exclusive animated stickers and reactions in multiplayer chat.

    F. Advanced Analytics (The "Pro" Stats)¶

    Deep dive into your performance data.

    • Extended History: View your entire workout history (Free users are limited to the last 10 sessions).
    • Performance Heatmaps: Visual breakdown of accuracy per lane/sector to identify weak spots (e.g., "You consistently miss low-left targets").

    G. Interface Customization¶

    Tailor the HUD to your preference.

    • HUD Skins: Ability to theme the in-game scoring UI (Combo Counter, Health Bar) with different styles (e.g., "Retro", "Minimalist").

    H. External Integrations (The "Connected Athlete")¶

    Sync your data with your fitness ecosystem.

    • Health Platform Sync: Automatic synchronization of workout data (Calories, Duration, Heart Rate) to external platforms like Strava, Apple Health, or Google Fit.
    • Data Export: Ability to download raw CSV logs of your workout history for personal analysis.

    I. Social Structures (Crews)¶

    Build your own community.

    • Crew Creation: Ability to found and manage a "Crew" (Guild/Clan). Includes a custom Tag (e.g., [NEON]), a private leaderboard, and management tools.
      • Note: Free players can join Crews, but only Supporters can create them.

    3. Philosophy on Restrictions¶

    We strictly adhere to a Free-to-Win policy.

    • No Gameplay Locking: We never lock Songs, Modes, or Maps behind the Supporter tag.
    • No Pay-to-Win: Supporters do not get gameplay advantages (e.g., higher scores or easier hit windows).
