# Creating Custom Avatars

Avatars in AeroBeat primarily support:

1. **Coach presentation**
2. **Athlete identity** in social or multiplayer contexts

This guide covers how to rig, import, and configure custom 3D characters.

## Scope note

Avatar customization remains a valid future-facing product area and fits the newer direction of controlled profile/cosmetics progression better than the older package-local gameplay asset model.
