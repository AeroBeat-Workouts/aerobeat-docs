# Gamification & Habit Building

AeroBeat should reward **consistency first**, not endless meta-system complexity.

For the launch product, the retention loop should stay narrow and honest:

- **Workout Points (WP)** as the single launch progression currency
- **simple weekly goals**
- **simple streaks** tied to consistency

That is enough to make progress visible without turning v1 into a premature live-service treadmill.

See also: [Account and Retention Feature Phasing](../../architecture/account-retention-phasing.md).

## V1 retention loop

### Workout Points

Workout Points remain the main progression currency in v1.

Their job is simple:

- reward completed workouts
- make progress visible
- support a modest set of official unlocks if content supply is ready

WP should **not** imply a large rotating store, premium-currency exchange, or battle-pass system at launch.

### Weekly goals

AeroBeat should let athletes pursue a simple weekly consistency target.

Recommended v1 examples:

- workout X days this week
- complete X sessions this week

The rule should be easy to understand and aligned with real workout habits.

### Streaks

Streaks should reinforce habit-building, not punish normal life.

Recommended v1 posture:

- prefer a **weekly consistency streak** over a brittle daily-login streak
- avoid paid streak repair or similar manipulative mechanics

## Deliberately deferred from v1

These may be valuable later, but they should not define the launch retention loop:

- global leaderboards
- social accountability graphs and crews
- premium currency
- battle passes
- seasonal progression tracks
- rotating shops

Those systems should be added only after the base workout loop, entitlement flows, and simple progression loop are proven.

## Explorer bonus

Variety goals in the current docs slice should encourage athletes to explore the retained gameplay features, especially Boxing and Flow, rather than referencing removed modes.
