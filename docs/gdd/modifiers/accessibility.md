# ♿ Accessibility Modifiers

Before starting a workout, athletes can enable accessibility focused modifiers to change their gameplay.

* **No Squats:** Disables `obstacles` that require you to `squat` down to avoid them in `boxing` or `flow` workouts.
* **No Knee Strikes:** Disables `knee strikes` targets in `boxing` workouts
* **No Leg Lifts:** Disables `leg_lift_left` and `leg_lift_right` beats in `flow` workouts
* **No Arrows:** Disables directional requirements in `boxing` and `flow` workouts
* **No Obstacles:** Disables obstacle/body-movement beats in `boxing` and `flow` workouts
* **No Trails & Warnings:** Disables `trail_*` and `warn_*` beats in `flow` workouts
* **Zen Mode:** Disables `targets` and `obstacles`, just enjoy the sounds and the sights
* **Hit Streak:** Enables the `hit streak` counter to be visible during gameplay.
* **Practice Mode:** Enables you to scrub a `timeline` while paused to jump to sections of the current song during a workout. `workout points` are not saved while enabled.
