# ♿ Accessibility Modifiers

Before starting a workout, athletes can enable accessibility-focused modifiers to change gameplay.

* **No Squats:** Disables `squat` prompts.
* **No Weaves:** Disables Boxing `weave_left` and `weave_right` prompts.
* **No Arrows:** Disables Flow arrow-direction validation while keeping timed cell-entry requirements.
* **No Obstacles:** Disables Boxing movement prompts such as `squat` / `weave_*` and disables Flow obstacle windows.
* **No Bombs:** Disables Flow bomb hazards.
* **No Guidance Objects:** Hides Flow guidance-only arcs/sliders and similar visual helpers.
* **Zen Mode:** Disables scoring pressure so the athlete can just enjoy the sounds and sights.
* **Hit Streak:** Enables the `hit streak` counter to be visible during gameplay.
* **Practice Mode:** Enables you to scrub a `timeline` while paused to jump to sections of the current song during a workout. `workout points` are not saved while enabled.
