## Asset Topology

We separate art assets into specialized repositories to facilitate modding and strict dependency management:

* **Skins:** Gameplay items (Gloves, Bats, Targets).
* **Avatars:** 3D Characters.
* **Cosmetics:** Accessories (Hats, Glasses).
* **Environments:** Levels and Lighting.
* **Assets:** Internal assets included with assembly builds, such as UI, 3D models, Sound, Fonts, etc. (Not for UGC).

## Logo Design

*   **Geometry**: Thick white geometric shapes
*   **Typography**: Clean sans-serif 
*   **Subtext**: Below the **AeroBeat** name - "Move To The Beat" in a smaller lighter weight font
*   **Color**: Pure White, no gradients or colors for the logo or text
*   **Spacing**: Symmetry and negative space used to create a clean, memorable, minimalistic icon

## Menu Design

*   **Flat Rounded Cards**: Minimalistic rounded cards with even spacing to the edge of all elements.
*   **Semi-Transparent Black**: A semi transparent black provides contrast while showing the background behind it
*   **Headers Above Cards**: Category header text elements appear above the card in bold white
