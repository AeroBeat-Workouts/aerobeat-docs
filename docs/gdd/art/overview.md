## Asset Topology

We separate art assets into specialized repositories to facilitate modding and strict dependency management:

* **Skins:** Gameplay items (Gloves, Bats, Targets).
* **Avatars:** 3D Characters.
* **Cosmetics:** Accessories (Hats, Glasses).
* **Environments:** Levels and Lighting.
* **Assets:** Internal assets included with assembly builds, such as UI, 3D models, Sound, Fonts, etc. (Not for UGC).
